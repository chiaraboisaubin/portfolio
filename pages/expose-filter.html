Context

To increase users' activity for the filtering feature of the shopping experience, precisely the product list page. However, it did evolve to look at the filtering experience throughout the enterprise and find ways to make the feature consistent and introduce horizontal filters.

User problems

People were not using filters throughout the CVS experience. Some feedback was gathered and users want a simple way to filter information. We sought to see how we can make the experience feel less overwhelming.

Short term project

  • Make specific features accessible by exposing a subset of information people look for the most.

Long term project

  • Explore the existing filter feature.
  • Redesign the filtering experience by creating solutions for consistency and clarity for users.

    • Introduce new components for a design system.

Initial goal

  • Determine why and where users were not using the filtering experience and create solutions.
  • Ensuring the experience is native to each platform.
  • Make global components and elements for the enterprise.

Desk research

Our analytics show that less than 10% of the users used the filters on the product list page and the loyalty sections. With the information gathered from a usability test in April 2021, the following was called out:

  • The repeating or too much information.
  • A lot of filters did not connect to what a person was looking for.
  • There is no ability to search through the filter.
  • Difficult to Navigate - specific on web mobile, iOS, and aOS.

Takeaways

Did we answer our questions?

We were able to restrict the information so it is easy to find the everyday information users were looking for.

Did we achieve our goals?

We figured out methods to implement the horizontal filter but did not learn why the user was not touching the current experience.

Next steps?

  • Meet weekly with teams that had a filter experience.
  • Organize when to conduct QA.
  • Do another user test with existing filter on product listing page.
  • Conduct card sorting session.
  • Strategize with the merchant and search team to create solutions for inputting types of filters.
  • Collaborate with other teams to implement the components throughout the CVS enterprise.
