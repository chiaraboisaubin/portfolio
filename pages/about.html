About Chiara

Hiya, my name is Chiara (chee-r-ah) , I also go by Chi (chee) . My pronouns are she/her/they/them. I am a UX/UI Designer, Product Designer, Digital Graphic Designer, Front End Developer, and Researcher.

I have been designing, researching, planner, and developing for users for over eight years, and I enjoy working on making tech sync with people's lives.

I admire human behavior when it comes to their interaction with technology. Learning from people's experiences and making solutions drives me to design intuitive user experiences while being authentic and creative and finding methods to make technology inclusive and accessible for people by thinking this throughout the design process.

I use my front-end development and digital design background to inform my views on improving accessibility issues with designs and development. I enjoy building wireframes and prototypes, conducting usability testing, and architecting user flows .

When I work, I think about the people first and how we can make their lives flexible and straightforward. I value collaboration and inclusion and bring a user-focused growth mindset daily.
